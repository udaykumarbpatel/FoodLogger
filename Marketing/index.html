The Problem
01

Calorie apps are exhausting.

Counting numbers turns eating into math homework. You stop logging. Then you stop caring.

02

Cloud diaries aren't private.

Your food habits live on someone else's server. Served back to you as ads. Or just gone when the startup shuts down.

03

Most apps do too much.

You just want to write down what you ate. Not manage a meal plan, track macros, and unlock achievements.

Features
📝

Text, Voice & Photo

Log a meal however feels natural. Switch between all three modes from the same sheet.

🏷

Auto Meal Detection

Breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack, dessert, beverage — auto-tagged. Override with one tap.

📊

Insights & Charts

8 chart types. 5 time periods. Top foods by frequency, daily activity, category breakdown, meal timing by hour, week-over-week comparison, monthly consistency heatmap, food search, and a streak stats card. See what you actually eat.

📅

Weekly Recap

Every Sunday, your week arrives as a notification. Tap it for a full six-page recap — category breakdowns, top foods, consistency score, and a shareable summary card. Generated entirely on your device.

🔥

Streak Tracking

Consecutive days logged. Shown in the Today banner every time you open the app.

🔒

100% On-Device

Zero network requests. Ever. Nothing you log leaves your phone.

📅

Calendar & Search

Browse any day on the full month grid. Search every entry you’ve ever logged.

✈️

Works Offline

No connection? No problem. FoodLogger has never needed the internet.
