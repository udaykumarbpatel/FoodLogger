Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Short version: FoodLogger collects nothing. Your food diary lives entirely on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your data — because we never receive it.

What data we collect

None. FoodLogger does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.

All data you enter — meal descriptions, photos, voice recordings, and app settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It never leaves your device.

On-device processing

Every feature in FoodLogger runs entirely on your device:

  • Voice logging — transcribed using Apple's on-device Speech framework (SFSpeechRecognizer)
  • Photo logging — classified using Apple's on-device Vision framework (VNClassifyImageRequest)
  • Category detection — processed locally using Apple's NaturalLanguage framework
  • Notifications — scheduled and delivered locally; no push notification server is used

Third-party services

FoodLogger uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services. There are zero network requests made by the app.

iCloud & backups

FoodLogger does not use iCloud sync. However, if you have iCloud backup enabled on your device, Apple may include app data as part of your standard device backup. This is handled entirely by iOS and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data export & deletion

You are in full control of your data:

  • Export — use Settings → Export Data to download a JSON copy of all your entries
  • Delete entries — swipe left on any entry in the app to delete it
  • Delete all data — deleting FoodLogger from your device removes all app data permanently

Children's privacy

FoodLogger does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. As described above, no data is collected at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here. Given the nature of the app (zero data collection), significant changes are unlikely.
